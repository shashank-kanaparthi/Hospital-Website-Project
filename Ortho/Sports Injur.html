Sports Injuries & Arthroscopy

Expert evaluation, precise diagnosis, and advanced repair of sports injuries—delivered through minimally invasive arthroscopic surgery for faster recovery and return to activity.

Comprehensive Sports Injury Care

Injury Evaluation

Advanced imaging and physical exams to accurately diagnose ligament tears, cartilage damage, and joint instability.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive procedures for ACL reconstruction, meniscus repair, rotator cuff tears, and other common sports injuries, using small incisions and high-definition cameras for precision[5][6].

Joint Preservation

Techniques to protect and restore damaged joints, preventing arthritis and maintaining long-term mobility for athletes.

Shoulder & Elbow Care

Treatment for dislocations, labral tears, and tendon injuries—common in throwing, racquet, and contact sports.

Foot & Ankle Treatment

Arthroscopic solutions for ankle sprains, cartilage lesions, and sports-related foot injuries.

Preventive Care

Customized injury prevention programs, warm-up routines, and biomechanical analysis for athletes of all levels.

Why Choose Arthroscopic Surgery?

Faster Recovery

Small incisions mean less tissue damage, reduced pain, and quicker return to daily activities—often days, not weeks[6].

Minimal Scarring

Tiny puncture wounds heal quickly and are barely visible, with excellent cosmetic results[6].

Lower Risk

Reduced risk of infection, bleeding, and complications compared to traditional open surgery[6].

Outpatient Care

Many procedures are done on an outpatient basis, allowing you to go home the same day[6].

Precision Diagnosis

High-definition cameras allow direct visualization of joint structures, leading to more accurate treatment[5].

Rehabilitation Support

Earlier mobilization and targeted physiotherapy help athletes regain strength and performance faster[7].
