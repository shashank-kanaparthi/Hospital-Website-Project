Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation

Regain mobility, reduce pain, and restore independence with tailored rehabilitation programs led by experienced physiotherapists at OrthoCare Hospital—your partner for recovery and wellness.

Comprehensive Rehabilitation Services

Post-Surgical Rehab

Specialized programs to restore function, strength, and mobility after orthopedic surgeries, joint replacements, or fractures.

Sports Injury Rehab

Customized therapy for athletes, helping you return to your sport stronger and safer, with injury prevention strategies.

Spinal Rehabilitation

Pain relief, posture correction, and functional training for back and neck conditions, including disc injuries and sciatica.

Neurological Rehab

Support for stroke, Parkinson’s, and other neurological conditions to enhance independence and quality of life.

Cardiac & Pulmonary Rehab

Supervised exercise and education for patients recovering from heart surgery or managing chronic lung diseases.

Pediatric & Geriatric Care

Age-appropriate therapy for children and seniors, focusing on development, mobility, and fall prevention.

Advanced Therapy Techniques

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques to improve joint and soft tissue mobility, reduce pain, and enhance healing.

Robotic Rehabilitation

Cutting-edge robotic devices and exoskeletons to accelerate recovery and restore natural movement patterns.

Electrotherapy

Use of electrical stimulation to relieve pain, reduce swelling, and promote muscle activity.

Aquatic Therapy

Low-impact exercises in warm water to improve strength, flexibility, and balance with minimized joint stress.

Therapeutic Exercise

Personalized exercise programs to rebuild strength, endurance, and coordination.

Patient Education

Empowering you with knowledge and self-management strategies for long-term health and injury prevention.
